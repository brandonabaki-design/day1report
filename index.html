Target Language & Structure

Observation: While compliance improved today, one flagged lesson noted a lack of clear structure and a failure to consistently use the target language (English) during instruction.

  • Require structured lesson outlines with explicit pacing for struggling teachers.

Pushing the Ceiling

Observation: Several lessons achieved full compliance ("All Observed") but received specific feedback requesting the inclusion of more challenging activities and extension tasks.
