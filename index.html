Key Areas for Improvement & Action Plan

Pacing & Teacher Talk Time

Observation: Several morning lessons struggled heavily with pacing. Excessive Teacher Talk Time (TTT) is causing lost learning time, leaving students waiting rather than actively engaging with tasks.

Suggested Action

  • Instruct teachers to use digital timers during the "Explain" phase to cap lecturing.
  • Shift structure to facilitate independent tasks earlier in the session.

Interactive Engagement

Observation: While afternoon sessions showed 100% compliance, observers noted the need to push the ceiling by adding more interactive elements and varied challenge levels (differentiation) to stimulate critical thinking.

Suggested Action

  • Require 2-3 specific higher-order discussion questions in weekly lesson plans.
  • Introduce digital tools that allow simultaneous, interactive student responses.

Peer Observation Cycles

Observation: Observers noted a stark contrast in delivery quality today, with strong commendations for classes like 4B Math and afternoon sessions, alongside critical flags in others.

Suggested Action

  • Formalize peer observations immediately. Struggling teachers should be scheduled to observe high-performing peers this week.
