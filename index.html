Key Areas for Improvement & Action Plan

Lesson Structure & Planning

Observation: Multiple lessons (Science, Art, General) were flagged as "very weak" or "lacking clear structure". This resulted in low student engagement and unachieved objectives. Content was occasionally noted as being below grade level.

Suggested Action

  • Mandatory lesson plan reviews by HODs 48 hours prior to delivery for flagged teachers.
  • Ensure English remains the medium of instruction across relevant subjects.

Classroom & Behavior Mgt.

Observation: Observers noted frequent interruptions and high teacher-talk-time. Quiet students were left out, while talkative students frequently disrupted the flow.

Suggested Action

  • Start each lesson with a strict 2-minute recap of online classroom rules and expectations.
  • Consistently use the mute function to manage interruptions and prompt quiet students specifically.

Higher-Order Questioning

Observation: While some teachers excel at questioning, several reports highlight that questions remained at a surface level, failing to promote deeper understanding or critical thinking.

Suggested Action

  • Teachers must include at least 3 scripted critical-thinking (HOTS) questions in their submitted lesson plans.
  • Increase challenge levels for early finishers.
