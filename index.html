Key Areas for Improvement & Action Plan

Task Differentiation

Observation: While support for SEN is mostly effective, multiple reports highlight that students are often given the same starting tasks. Extensions are provided only after task completion.

Suggested Action

  • Provide tiered activities allowing students to start at varying challenge levels.
  • Host a mini-CPD (Continual Professional Development) on creating differentiated entry points.

Digital Tool Diversity

Observation: There is an over-reliance on basic tools like the Zoom/Teams chatbox for monitoring student work, which is inefficient for formative assessment.

Suggested Action

  • Mandate the use of collaborative boards (e.g., Padlet, ClassKick, Whiteboard.fi).
  • Share a "Digital Tool of the Week" highlighting effective online monitoring techniques.

Lesson Visibility & Management

Observation: Lesson objectives sometimes disappear from the screen during independent work. Additionally, time management during "Explore" phases occasionally runs long.

Suggested Action

  • Ensure objectives and success criteria are pinned in the chat or embedded on every slide.
  • Use visible digital timers for independent tasks to maintain lesson pacing.
